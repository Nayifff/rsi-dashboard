Stock Table — Swing Screener

Fundamentals (Finnhub) + multi-timeframe RSI (1h + 4h) · ranked by Swing Score · how it works

Add / remove any ticker. Adding one auto-fetches just that ticker — no need to re-run the rest. Run fetches only new or stale rows (older than 10 min); ↻ All force-refreshes everything.

Columns — Price & daily % change, Market Cap, Avg Volume (10-day, a liquidity gauge), P/E (all from Finnhub), plus RSI on the 1h and 4h timeframes.

Swing Score = 100 − (0.6 × RSI4h + 0.4 × RSI1h). Lower RSI on both timeframes ⇒ more oversold ⇒ higher score ⇒ stronger long-entry setup. 4h is weighted higher as the primary swing trend; 1h times the entry. The table is ranked by this score.

Entry zone: RSI ≤ Entry (oversold). Exit zone: RSI ≥ Exit (overbought). A filled dot = that timeframe is currently in the zone.

Signals — STRONG BUY 1h & 4h both entry · BUY ZONE either entry · EXIT ZONE either exit · TAKE PROFIT both exit · NEUTRAL otherwise.

RSI tries Finnhub /indicator first; if your Finnhub plan lacks technical indicators it falls back to your Twelve Data key. 4h RSI is derived by aggregating 60-min candles. Educational tool — not financial advice.
