Swing Scanner — Top 10 Liquid Tech

Multi-timeframe RSI (1h + 4h) · ranked by Swing Score · how the score works

Universe — the 10 most liquid US-listed mega-cap technology stocks (NVDA, TSLA, AAPL, AMZN, MSFT, META, AMD, GOOGL, AVGO, NFLX). High dollar volume keeps spreads tight and slippage low.

Swing Score = 100 − (0.6 × RSI4h + 0.4 × RSI1h). Lower RSI on both timeframes ⇒ more oversold ⇒ higher score ⇒ stronger long-entry setup. The 4h is weighted higher as the primary swing trend; the 1h times the entry.

Entry zone: RSI ≤ Entry (oversold — look to enter long). Exit zone: RSI ≥ Exit (overbought — take profit / exit). Shown separately for 1h and 4h.

Signals — STRONG BUY 1h & 4h both in entry · BUY ZONE either in entry · EXIT ZONE either in exit · TAKE PROFIT both in exit · NEUTRAL otherwise.
