Cost & scaling

  • Credits: cheap mode = 1/stock, accurate (native 4h) = 2/stock. Free tier = 8/min, 800/day.
  • Scanning 50 stocks (cheap) ≈ 50 credits ≈ ~6 min on free. 500 stocks ≈ 500 credits — needs a paid plan (Grow, ~$29/mo: higher per-minute, no daily cap).
  • No database: candles/RSI are cached in localStorage; stale rows (older than TTL) refetch, fresh ones don't.
  • True scheduled scanning of thousands of stocks 24/7 would need a small backend (serverless cron + store). That can't run on GitHub Pages — this page is the browser-only version.
